Who are we?

We are a group of computer science students at the Technion institute of technology. In our artificial intelligence project, we tried to predict movies ratings according to the movies features and based on previous ratings for movies in the IMDb database.

How do we predict the rating?

We collected data on 15,428 movies. We tried diffrent machine learning regressors and we run many expriments so we can decide which regressor to use. And we choose for our web the regressor that gave the best results.

What features should you know about the movie that you are trying to predict?

In order to use the algorithm that we built you will have to know some basic information about the movie. The information that is required is the following:

  • The date of publishing the movie.
  • The genre of the movie.
  • The duration of the movie.
  • The countries that took part in the production.
  • The langueges that are spoken in the movie.
  • The name of the production company.
  • The budget for the movie.
  • The names of the two main actors in the movie.
  • The name of the director of the movie.
